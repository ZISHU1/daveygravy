About Me

I'm Davey Gravy — a commercial photographer and videographer based in Calgary, Canada. Through my life, I’ve always found beauty in the world around me. I began my creative career in visual arts by attending and graduating from Alberta College of Art + Design. Since then, I’ve found my home in photography and videography. I’ve been fortunate to travel locally and abroad with clients to share stories and capture beauty.

I aim to tell authentic stories through everything I do — creating natural-feeling content with a hint of documentary style and a unique, meaningful approach. Being trained as a multidisciplinary artist has encouraged me to explore different mediums, overcome challenges, and take risks to create something memorable.
